Required Images for Google Play Store:

1. Screenshots: Add at least 2 PNG images to images/phoneScreenshots/
   - Size: Various, but typically 1080x1920 or similar for phones
   - Show gameplay, menu, etc.

2. featureGraphic.png: 1024x500 pixels
   - Promotional banner for the store listing

3. icon.png: 512x512 pixels (already copied from app resources)

Optional:
- promoGraphic.png: 180x120 pixels
- tvBanner.png: 1280x720 pixels (if supporting TV)

Please add these images to complete the metadata.
