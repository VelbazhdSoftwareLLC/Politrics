Politrics is an exciting board game inspired by Chess, designed for strategic minds who enjoy deep tactical gameplay.

**Game Objective:**
Win by scoring 100 points, the maximum on a single board. Accumulate points across multiple boards to claim victory.

**Ways to Win:**
1. **Line Formation:** Get 5 figures in a line (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal) that are safe from your opponent.
2. **Beat the President:** Capture your opponent's President and score based on your highest value combination.
3. **Incapacitate Opponent:** Leave your opponent with insufficient figures to form a line of 5.

**Board Layout:**
- 81 squares in a 9x9 grid
- Inner 7x7 playing field
- Scoring squares in blue shades (1-6 points)
- Retirement squares in red shades

**Figures:**
Two sets of 17 figures each (dark and light), each with unique movement and capture rules.

**Scoring System:**
Strategic placement on scoring squares earns points. The center square acts as a multiplier for enhanced scoring.
